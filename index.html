How to Play

Game Overview

Imposter Word Brain is a social deduction game where players try to identify the "imposters" among them based on word association clues.

Setup

  1. Select the number of players (3-10) and imposters (1-3)
  2. Enter names for all players
  3. Start the game and pass the device around

Word View Phase

  • Each player secretly views their word
  • Regular players all get the SAME word
  • Imposters get SIMILAR but different words
  • Keep your word secret from others!

Game Rounds (3 Rounds)

  • On your turn, say ONE word related to your word
  • Try to blend in if you're an imposter
  • Regular players try to identify imposters based on word choices

Discussion & Voting

  • After 3 rounds, discuss and debate
  • As a group, decide if imposters won or lost
  • The losing side receives Truth or Dare challenges
